Additional Information

Membership Levels:

Non-Profit ($100)

This base level is displayed in our directory and gets a table every year for our Conference.

Bronze ($200)

This second tier with have the benifit of everything at the base level and receive luncheon tickets to our yearly conference. They will be included in all social media posts by the Chamber as well.

Silver ($400)

In addition to the first two levels, Silver level memberships have the opportunity to be added to any or all advertising included with the sponsorship of the Chamber.

Gold ($1000)

The top tier of membership with the chamber includes of all of our resources that have been mentioned in the lower tiers. This top tier will also receive business trainings 4 times a year. One on one mentorship will be provided for your business to create the highest level of suppport. Our annual conference will also provide you with a reserved table at our luncheon and a table at the opening of the fair.
