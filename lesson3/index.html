Site Purpose

The Weather Scoop is a site where visitors can come and find the weather for their area in South Eastern Idaho. The site will hold current weather for the participating areas, as well as be a community and regional site that includes current relevant news, current events in the areas, and other relevant content. Photos realating to the areas and their current weather patterns can be viewed. 6 main pages with hold the information for 1 home page, 3 South Eastern Cities, a storm center page, and a photo gallery.
