This site is for those in the south eastern idaho looking for weather and current information and events on the area. These people can either be traveling through or be local residents.

Persona 1: These people are traveling through the area and are looking for current weather conditions and events. They are middle aged, traveling by car long distances, looking for local attractions such as Lava Hot Springs, middle class, little to large amounts of higher education. Family oriented people looking for things to do in a group. Have little knowledge of weather.

Persona 2: These people live in the area and are looking for current weather conditions and events. These people are middle aged. They are Family oriented, and keep an eye on current news and events. These people have reasons to travel around the local areas and are keeping an eye on weather.

Scenario 1: A family is travling from out of state and looking for weather as they pass through to northern Idaho and Montana. They need a site to fill in the gap of information. They don't want to stop and want to know the fastest way though with the best weather. They look at maps and pictures to support this desire. They check the map and weather for the area they plan to drive through.

Scenario 2: A couple is travling to the area and are looking for current weather information and events in the area. The plan to stop in the area for a time on their trip and are looking for ideas.

Scenario 3: A middle aged man living in south eastern idaho has decided to stop receiving his newspapers but still wants local news and weather. He travels to the sight daily to get this information and to look up local information.

Scenario 4: A local family is looking to travel to another town represented in the site. They navigate to the site to plan a family gathering. They check the weather and for events happening during their gathering.

Scenario 5: An older couple who live in the area in the summer and live down south in the winter use this site to follow the weather to determine a good time to travel. They check for local news that might cause problems with their travel. They are also able to check this while they are alway from their home to know when to have someone check on their house through the winter based on weather.
